About This Project

This site collects writing on food as a lens to understand culture, identity, labor, migration, and social change. It is aimed at readers interested in anthropology, sociology, history, and food studies.

Posts include essays, annotated bibliographies, teaching resources, and reflections on research methods and fieldwork related to food.
